Anchors Exercise

Make use of everything you learned in Anchors tutorial to create a Blog page (named links.html) with the following information:

  1. Blog title

  2. Links to your Top 3 Favorite Movies (Briefly tell why you love each movie in a paragraph)

  3. Links to your Top 3 Favorite Songs (Briefly tell why you love each song in a paragraph)

  4. Your Social links (Choose two of the following)
    • A link to your Facebook timeline
    • A link to your Twitter profile
    • A link to your Google+ profile

  5. Links to your two previous exercises
    • A link to your text-formatting-2.html
    • A link to your list-2.html

  6. Links from your two previous exercises to this page (Edit your previous exercises and add a link to go to this page)
    • A link from your text-formatting-2.html
    • A link from your list-2.html

* Note that you have to imitate the web page exactly how it looks. Make use of your learnings from your previous exercises (paragraphs, headings, lists).
